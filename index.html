About Dark And Lovely

Dark & Lovely was introduced nearly 50 years ago as the first hair-color product formulated specifically for Black women.

Over the years, Dark & Lovely has grown to include a complete collection of shampoos, fade-resistant hair colors, relaxers, deep-conditioning treatments, stylers, and hair foods.

Now, decades later, Fade and Resist, its color collection that is sold in nearly 80,000 retailers, continues to be one of the brand's top sellers.

The campaign was a new emphasis and an opportunity to speak to a younger consumer who was just being introduced to the legacy brand.

The campaign featured Gen Z and transgender talent who really laid the groundwork for the brand's now-partnership with actress and activist Storm Reid.
